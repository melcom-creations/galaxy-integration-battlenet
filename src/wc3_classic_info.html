Warcraft III Classics – How to Play

GOG Galaxy · Battle.net Plugin · Setup Guide

⚠
You must own and have registered a valid product key for:
  • Warcraft® III: Reign of Chaos®
  • Warcraft® III: The Frozen Throne®
Without a registered key, neither download nor play is possible.
Step 1 – Redeem your key (if you haven't already)
🌐 battle.net/shop › Profile icon (top right) › Account Settings › Account Overview › Redeem a Code
Enter your product key there. Both games will then appear in your account under Games & Subscriptions → Classic Games.
Step 2 – Choose how you want to play
Route A · Standalone Download the classic standalone launchers
1
Open the Battle.net website
Go to us.shop.battle.net and sign in with your Battle.net account.
2
Open Account Settings
Click your profile icon in the top right → Account Settings.
3
Games & Subscriptions → Classic Games
In the left sidebar click Games & Subscriptions, then scroll down to the Classic Games section.
4
Download both games
Click Download for Warcraft® III: Reign of Chaos® and Warcraft® III: The Frozen Throne® individually. Each installs its own launcher and runs independently of the Battle.net app.
or
Route B · Battle.net Client Play Legacy TFT 1.29 inside the Battle.net client under Warcraft III
1
Open the Battle.net launcher
Not installed yet? Download it at download.battle.net/en-us/desktop.
2
Select Warcraft III
Click Warcraft III in the left sidebar of the Battle.net client.
3
Switch the version
Under Warcraft III, next to the Play button, there is a small dropdown arrow. Click it and select Warcraft III – Legacy TFT 1.29.
4
Install Legacy TFT 1.29
After selecting it from the dropdown under Warcraft III, the Play button changes to Install. Click it to download and install the Legacy TFT 1.29 build.
5
Hit Play
Once installed, press Play — the Battle.net client will start the classic version (Reign of Chaos + The Frozen Throne combined as Legacy TFT 1.29).
💡 Note for GOG Galaxy: Until the standalone classic launchers are installed on your PC, Warcraft® III: Reign of Chaos® and Warcraft® III: The Frozen Throne® will appear as not installed in GOG Galaxy. That is intentional — use one of the two routes above to play them.
